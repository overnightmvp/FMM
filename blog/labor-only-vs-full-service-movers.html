Labor-Only vs. Full-Service Movers: Which One Saves You More in Fort Myers?

When you're planning a move in Fort Myers, you'll quickly discover two very different price points: full-service movers at $900–$1,400 for a 2-bedroom, and labor-only movers at $400 for the same job.

What's the difference? And which one is right for you?

The Short Answer

Choose labor-only if: You can arrange a rental truck (U-Haul, Penske) or have a PODS container.
Choose full-service if: You want someone else to handle literally everything including the truck.

What Each Option Includes

Full-Service Movers

  • Their own truck (included in price)
  • 2–4 professional movers
  • Loading and unloading
  • Basic furniture protection
  • May include packing (often extra)

Average cost in Fort Myers: $900–$1,400 for a 2-bedroom local move

Labor-Only Movers

  • 2–3 professional movers
  • All equipment (dollies, blankets, straps)
  • Loading and/or unloading
  • You provide the truck or container

Average cost in Fort Myers: $400 for a 2-bedroom local move

The Math: Why Labor-Only Wins for Most Moves

For a typical Fort Myers 2-bedroom move:

Cost ItemFull-ServiceLabor-Only
Moving company$1,000–$1,200$400
Truck rental (U-Haul, 1 day)Included$80–$120

Your savings—$480–$720

You save $480–$720 on a single 2-bedroom move. For a 3-bedroom move, the savings are even larger.

When Full-Service Makes More Sense

  1. You're moving out of state — full-service is the standard for long-distance
  2. You have very large or unusual items (baby grand piano, custom pool table) — some specialty items require specific trucks and equipment
  3. You want zero involvement — some people want to pay more to hand the entire job to professionals
  4. Corporate relocation — many companies pay for full-service as part of employee relocation

When Labor-Only Is the Clear Winner

  1. You're moving within Fort Myers or Southwest Florida — local moves don't need the full-service overhead
  2. You already rented a U-Haul — you're halfway there already
  3. You have a PODS or storage container — labor-only is designed for this
  4. You're moving 1–3 bedrooms — the price difference is dramatic
  5. You're a seasonal resident — moving in/out of a condo? Save hundreds

Full-service movers often include packing, or charge extra for it. Labor-only movers typically don't pack — but we offer packing as an add-on for $100/room with supplies included.

If you want everything handled, labor-only + packing add-on is still usually cheaper than full-service.

The Verdict

For the vast majority of Fort Myers moves:

  • Quality is equal when you hire a licensed, insured, reviewed provider
