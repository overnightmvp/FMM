What Affects the Price

The number of bedrooms (or more accurately, the number of items to move) is the primary driver. A 3-bedroom with minimal furniture costs less than a 2-bedroom packed to the ceiling.

Most labor-only services price based on home size and include a set number of hours. If the job runs longer, overage is charged hourly (typically $75â€“$120/hour per mover, or $100â€“$200/hour for the crew).
